Quote of the day


If you have want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun
Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam
To Shine is to be at the pinnacle or peak of your desired field of interest or whatever it is that you want to achieve and Dr. Kalam believes that to ‘Shine’ like that, first you need to have a ‘Burning Desire’ or ‘Spirit’ for it which would propel you to work day in and day out consistently for your goal.

I'd rather be optimistic and wrong than pessimistic and right
Elon musk
Because you think in a negative way, you’ll experience loss of charm and happiness in you. And in such case, you will start creating hype about your behavior unknowingly. But being positive gives a ray of happiness and hope(at least for a certain time). So, even you’re wrong, keep your spirit high because this ‘optimistic’ failure will teach you a lot.

It is not in the stars to hold our destiny but in ourselves
William Shakespeare
We chose our own destiny. To either succeed or to fail, it is your fault (unless you have friends that rub off on you, then it's 1/4 their fault too). I do not believe in fate and for that reason, destiny is in your hands to shape and mold into your own, unique future. Be free and defy your standards.
